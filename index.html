About Me

Icy Mae is a boundary-pushing artist who builds songs like weather systems: slow pressure, sudden lightning, and emotional release. Their sound blends crisp percussion, cinematic synths, and intimate vocals into a style that feels both futuristic and deeply human.

On stage, every set is designed as a visual and sonic journey. Frosted textures, vibrant light movement, and crowd-first performance create an experience that is immersive from the first drop to the final note.

Career

From underground warehouse sets to sold-out headline nights, Icy Mae has grown through constant reinvention. Their debut cycle established a signature cold-pop identity, while recent releases expanded into bigger hooks, harder drums, and collaborative production with international electronic artists.

Career milestones include festival main-stage appearances, sync placements in indie films, and a rotating audiovisual show concept that evolves city by city. The mission stays the same: build songs that move people physically and emotionally.

  • Debut EP charted in three independent streaming playlists during launch week.
  • Featured on two international festival lineups in one year.
  • Collaborated with producers from Seoul, Berlin, and Sao Paulo.
  • Reached one million cumulative streams across major platforms.
